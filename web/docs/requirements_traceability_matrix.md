# Requirements Traceability Matrix — CHMS Web Prototype

| # | Requirement | Implemented by | Verified by |
|---|---|---|---|
| R1 | A student cannot hold two active/waitlisted seats in the same section, nor enroll in two sections of the same course. | `registrationRules.checkDuplicateEnrollment` | `blackBox/equivalencePartitioning.test.js` ("duplicate enrollment" describe block), `blackBox/decisionTable.test.js` (Row 0), `unit/validators.test.js` (`checkDuplicateEnrollment` describe block), `whiteBox/basisPathCoverage.test.js` (Path 0), `integration/registrationScenarios.test.js` |
| R2 | A full section without waitlisting either rejects the student or offers a waitlist seat. | `registrationService.register` (capacity/waitlist branch) | `blackBox/decisionTable.test.js` (Rows 5-7), `blackBox/boundaryValueAnalysis.test.js` (capacity boundary), `whiteBox/basisPathCoverage.test.js` (Paths 5-7), `whiteBox/branchCoverage.test.js` (`section full?` / `allowWaitlist` branches), `integration/registrationScenarios.test.js` |
| R3 | A student's total active credit hours must stay within their standing- and overload-aware maximum (18 standard / 14 probation / 21 with overload approval). | `registrationRules.checkCreditLimit`, `maxAllowedCredits`, `constants/creditPolicy.js` | `unit/validators.test.js` (`checkCreditLimit`/`maxAllowedCredits`), `blackBox/equivalencePartitioning.test.js` ("maximum credit limits"), `blackBox/boundaryValueAnalysis.test.js` (17/18/19 boundary), `blackBox/decisionTable.test.js` (Row 3) |
| R4 | A student's final registration must meet the full-time minimum (12 credits). | `registrationRules.checkMinimumCredits`, `registrationService.validateFinalRegistration` | `unit/validators.test.js`, `blackBox/boundaryValueAnalysis.test.js` (11/12/13 boundary), `whiteBox/branchCoverage.test.js` (`below minimum` branch), `integration/registrationScenarios.test.js` |
| R5 | A course's prerequisites must all be in the student's completed-courses set before registering (including before waitlisting). | `registrationRules.checkPrerequisites` | `unit/validators.test.js`, `blackBox/equivalencePartitioning.test.js` ("prerequisite validation"), `blackBox/boundaryValueAnalysis.test.js` (all-vs-all-but-one), `blackBox/decisionTable.test.js` (Rows 2 & 6) |
| R6 | A student cannot register for a section whose meeting times overlap a section they're already enrolled in. | `registrationRules.checkScheduleConflict`, `validators/schedule.js` (`timeSlotsOverlap`, `sectionsConflict`) | `unit/validators.test.js` (`checkScheduleConflict`), `blackBox/equivalencePartitioning.test.js` ("scheduling consistency"), `blackBox/boundaryValueAnalysis.test.js` (touching vs. 1-minute overlap), `blackBox/decisionTable.test.js` (Row 4) |
| R7 | The catalog itself must be internally consistent: no two sections may share a room or instructor at overlapping times. | `catalogService.validateScheduleConsistency` | `whiteBox/statementCoverage.test.js` (room+instructor+clean-pair case), `blackBox/equivalencePartitioning.test.js` ("catalog-wide" cases), `integration/registrationScenarios.test.js` (admin catalog management) |
| R8 | Dropping a section frees the seat and, if others are waitlisted, promotes the next *eligible* one (re-checking credit limit and schedule conflict at promotion time), skipping anyone no longer eligible. | `registrationService.drop`, `registrationService.promoteFromWaitlist` | `whiteBox/basisPathCoverage.test.js` (D1-D7), `whiteBox/branchCoverage.test.js` (`candidate document missing`, `candidate ineligible` branches), `blackBox/equivalencePartitioning.test.js` ("correctness of enrollment operations"), `integration/registrationScenarios.test.js` |
| R9 (data integrity, supporting) | Unknown course/section/student ids raise a clear, typed error rather than an opaque failure. | `catalogService.getCourse`, `catalogService.getSection`, `catalogService.addSection`, `registrationService.getStudent` | `whiteBox/statementCoverage.test.js`, `whiteBox/branchCoverage.test.js` (`student not found` branch), `unit/models.test.js` |
| R10 (supporting) | Only authenticated users may register/drop/finalize; only admins may manage the catalog or view the consistency report. | `middleware/auth.js` (`requireAuth`, `requireRole`), `controllers/authController.js` | `integration/registrationScenarios.test.js` (auth flow: signup/login, wrong password, duplicate email, missing token, wrong role) |
